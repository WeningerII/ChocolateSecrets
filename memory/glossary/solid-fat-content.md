# Solid-Fat Content (SFC)

**Definition.** The fraction of fat that is crystalline (solid) at a given
temperature — governs snap, melt, and mouthfeel of chocolate/confections.

**In this codebase.** An objective/constraint in the physics engine
(`src/services/foodScience/`), consumed by [[recipe-physics-engine]] and the
formulation optimizer.

**Related.** [[tempering]] · [[recipe-physics-engine]]
