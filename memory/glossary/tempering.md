# Tempering

**Definition.** Controlled heating/cooling of chocolate to crystallize cocoa
butter into the stable Form V polymorph — giving snap, gloss, and shelf stability.

**In this codebase.** A target of the formulation/texture engine; modelled via
[[solid-fat-content]] and process steps in `src/services/foodScience/` and
`src/hooks/useRecipePhysics.ts`. See [[recipe-physics-engine]].

**Related.** [[solid-fat-content]] · [[recipe-physics-engine]]
